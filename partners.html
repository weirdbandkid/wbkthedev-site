D.E.S.RP


Department of Emergency Services - Role Play Community (D.E.S.RP) is multi-platform community based off of role playing the first-responder life roles, now this does not mean you must choose a first-responder role to become a part of our community - we have many positions inside and outside of role play to fit your interest and needs. Although we label as a Community, we like to refer to ourselves as a Family, through our close friendships to our long adventures. D.E.S.RP was formed in August, 2018, meaning our Family is over two years old! We have many unique members who serve their real life community through many services such as Law Enforcement, Medical Services, Fire Services, Radio Communications and even Military Personnel! Having these said people with their experience ensures we are providing a realistic role play for each and every member who has the opportunity to join D.E.S.RP. The start & end goal of D.E.S is to 'bring in people from all over together to serve San Andreas in a fun, yet realistic visual'. 

We hope to see you in the field of role play, come and join us! While you're here, check out some of our in-game photos below!
